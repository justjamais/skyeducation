Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Sky-Edu Academy AB (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a mobile app company based in Malmö, Sweden. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website, services, or apps.

1. Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Contact and identity information (e.g. name, email, company) when you get in touch or use our services
  • Usage data (e.g. how you use our website or apps, device type, general location)
  • Technical data (e.g. IP address, browser type, cookies and similar technologies)

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services and mobile apps
  • Respond to your requests and communicate with you
  • Analyse usage and improve our products and marketing
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Legal basis (EU/EEA)

Where applicable under EU/EEA law, we process personal data based on consent, contract performance, legitimate interests, or legal obligation, as appropriate.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with service providers (e.g. hosting, analytics) who assist our operations, and when required by law or to protect our rights.

We use third-party API services, including fal.ai, to provide certain features of our platform. When you use these features, the prompts and images you provide may be sent to these services for the purpose of generating videos, which are then delivered back to you. We do not store this content on our own servers beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

5. Data retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, to data portability, and to object or withdraw consent. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Cookies and tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies for functionality and analytics. You can adjust your browser settings to manage cookies.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at peakasoagency@gmail.com or Sky-Edu Academy AB, Malmö, Sweden.
